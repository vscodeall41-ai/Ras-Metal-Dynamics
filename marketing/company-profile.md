# Ras Metal Dynamics — Company Profile

**Overview**
Ras Metal Dynamics is a Cape Town-based steel fabrication company specialising in custom structural and architectural metalwork. With over 12 years of hands-on experience, we deliver high-quality steel solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial projects across the Western Cape.

**Core Capabilities**
- Structural steel and heavy fabrication
- Custom gates, balustrades and fencing
- Staircases and architectural metalwork
- Pergolas, carports and outdoor structures
- Site welding and full installation

**Positioning**
We are the fabricator architects and contractors call when the steelwork needs to be done properly — on time, to specification, and finished to a high standard.

**Credentials**

- 12+ years operating in the Western Cape
- Full fabrication and installation capability
